Models
===========

    >>> convis.models.<tab>
                      L
                      LN
                      LNCascade
                      LNLN
                      LNFDSNF
                      McIntosh
                      Retina
                      ...

You can also have a look at :ref:`doc-models` for a a list of the available models.
